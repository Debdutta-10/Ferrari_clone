Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939. It is one of the world's most recognizable and prestigious automotive brands, known for its high-performance sports cars and for it's racing heritage. Ferrari has a rich racing history and has been involved in Formula One (F1) racing since the inception of the championship in 1950. The company has achieved great success in F1, winning numerous championships and becoming the most successful team in the sport's history.Ferrari is renowned for producing iconic and highly coveted sports cars. Some of the notable Ferrari models include the Ferrari 250 GTO, F40, F50, Enzo Ferrari, LaFerrari, and the recent SF90 Stradale. These cars are revered for their performance, design, and exclusivity.
